Marcos Museum

Sarrat is the birthplace of the late Philippine President Ferdinand Marcos. The Marcos Museum is housed in the Edralin house where Marcos was born on September 11, 1917 to Mariano and Josefa Edralin Marcos, both school teachers from well-to-do families. Marcos lived here until 8 years old when his family moved to Batac.
